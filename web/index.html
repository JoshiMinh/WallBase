Dynamic Design

Advanced Theming & Customization

Experience true Material 3 implementation. Switch between Light, Dark, Follow System, and AMOLED Black modes. Personalize your experience with a curated palette of UI accent colors or define your own custom shades.

Infinite Sources

Extensive Wallpaper Engines

Browse through millions of high-resolution images from the most popular sources on the web. From minimalist photography to digital art, WallBase connects you to the best repositories including Wallhaven, Unsplash, Reddit, and more.

Smart Automation

Seamless Background Refresh

Keep your home screen alive with automated wallpaper rotation. Define your favorite sources, set a refresh interval, and let WallBase handle the rest. Built with efficient background workers to ensure minimal battery impact.
